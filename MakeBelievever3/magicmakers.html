Magic Makers

Lesley Lina

Creative Director

Lesley is an actress, host, storyteller, playwright, teacher, and director. Teacher Lesley loves to weave colorful and touching tales that bring the young and young-at-heart to a place of magic, imagination, and self-discovery.

An honor graduate of the Ateneo de Manila University, Lesley can be seen in independent films and hosting on OShopping in between trips to fairyland. She is also a proud mother and wife.

Maruja Calixto

Design Director

Maruja, or Teacher Niña, is an honor graduate of Assumption College with a degree in Interior Design. A talented artist, Teacher Niña is not only a production designer, make-up artist, illustrator, and puppeteer, she is also a beloved storyteller, host, and teacher.

It is through her direction and vision that innovations in Make Believe crafts, costumes, puppets and event styling are developed.
