Since 2006, Make Believe has lovingly crafted magical experiences for children and families through workshops, parties, events, and shows. We’ve come a long way since we started as a humble community-based workshop group, and we are amazed at where our work has taken us thus far.

We believe that magic can be found with the right guidance, some ingenuity, and a zest for fun. See where the magic has taken us as we share our dreams to enrich others through imaginative play, theater, and the creative arts. Browse our site, learn more about us and our work, and write to us, please! For our greatest joy is making magic with your children, and with you.
