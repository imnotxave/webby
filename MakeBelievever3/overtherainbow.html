Over the Rainbow

Over the Rainbow: The Make Believe CSR Program

We believe in sharing the magic with those in need of it, and in collaborating with organizations that feel the same. In 2013, Make Believe launched the program Somewhere Over the Rainbow to use creativity and artistry to bring light, hope, and joy to children affected by poverty, sickness and natural disasters. Want to bring some Make Believe magic to cheer up those in need? We would love to partner with you.

Services offered:

Storytelling and entertainment for children

Confidence building workshops

Personal empowerment through the arts
