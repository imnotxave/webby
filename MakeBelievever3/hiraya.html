Hiraya

We believe in celebrating Filipino stories, culture, and artistry.

Meaning vision and imagination in Filipino, Hiraya by Make Believe is focused on introducing the current generation to the richness and beauty of Filipino stories, games, and culture with the aim of cultivating within them a deep love for and pride in their heritage. Maria, Ang Binibini Ng Bibingka

Young Maria comes from a long line of Bibingka vendors but is kept out of the kitchen by well-meaning parents who desire a very different life for her. One day, the family business is threatened by an automated bibingka maker. Her family is discouraged, but Maria does not lose hope. Maria, ang Binibini ng Bibingka is a sweet story about identity, family tradition, and the balance between finding your place in family history, while creating a story of your own.

First staged under the auspices of the Philippine Pediatric Society and the BGC Arts Centre for the BGC Arts Festival 2017, Maria, Ang Binibini ng Bibingka is a bilingual play that serves as a fun introduction to Filipino language and culture. Play is primarily in English with Filipino vocabulary introduced and unlocked through dialogue and song.

The show is available for tours and private bookings.
